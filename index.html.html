SOFTWARE PROJECTS

SCAFFOLDMAKER

I am currently developing a 3D modelling of anatomy to the 3D anatomy software, "Scaffoldmaker" in Python. This project has been developed under Auckland Bioengineering Institute to exhibit a 3D model of anatomy in various species (e.g. human, mouse, rat, pig, etc.) The applications of these 3D models are simulation, visualisation, and data registration. One of our famous works is the pounding heart. We can mimic the pounding heart in specific subjects and register research data on our 3D heart model to visualise its data and where exactly the specimen was extracted.

The project involved many skills such as computational modelling, finite element method, anatomy, research,data processing and analysis.
